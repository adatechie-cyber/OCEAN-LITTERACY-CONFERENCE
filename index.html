About the Conference

The International Conference on Fostering Ocean Literacy through Informal Marine Education Programs brings together leading scientists, educators, policymakers, and practitioners to share knowledge, innovative approaches, and best practices in marine education.

This year's theme focuses on the critical role of informal education in promoting ocean literacy and fostering stewardship of marine environments. Through keynote presentations, workshops, and networking sessions, participants will explore strategies for engaging diverse audiences in ocean conservation.

We are offering both virtual and in-person participation options to ensure broad accessibility for our international community of ocean advocates and educators.
